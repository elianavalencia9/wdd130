Home

Welcome to Elipostres! We are so happy that you are visiting our website! Here you will be pleased to find beautiful, delicious and funny cakes and other desserts that most people like to eat not only during a certain type of meeting but also any type of occasion. You know that when it’s about eating, the stomach has any excuse to to call us! We started our business due to a tradition of our gran-grand-mother who liked to make cakes and sell them to her neighbors, she was a good dealer! In these days, we are following that tradition and we hope that it can remain alive to the next generations!

Blog

No matter your age, we know that you like to eat products of good quality and taste and also adding to that features, the good presentation of the product, but you may be wondering about the price of it, right? But in that case, don’t worry about that, because our prices are thought for your wallet! And we also want you know that if you are a person who follow a certain type of diet (vegan, keto, glucose free, etc.), you can tell us about that and we can make you a product that fit your necessities. But despite that our product are good quality and good taste! We want you to have a good experience exploring our website, so we designed it in an easy way that you can navigate through it and find the things that you like and need at your fingertips. We don’t have an app yet but we hope to have one very soon, so your purchases will be easier!

Your wishes are orders

In this website, you will find products for any occasion, ranking from birthdays to any special day or occasion you would like to have them. You only have to peek our gallery photo and you can see which one you would like to pick and we will make it that for you, but If you have another idea in your mind, you only have to contact us in the contact link of this page and we will be very happy to help you, or you can also follow us on our social media and you can be updated about the current products we are making to our clients. We don’t have a physical store yet, so all our sales and orders are online. If you need something more specialized we can meet with you through a zoom meeting or we can schedule a day when we can meet with each other at a specific place. Almost every day we publish a new product made and delivered to one of our loyal customers. Our social media links are located at the bottom-right of this page. You can also find there discounts and surprises if you start following us! If you need a cake, please contact us on time, that help us to work in a good way for the elaboration of your product. If you have any questions or feedback, you can write us and we will be happy to answer them, that will help us grow and very soon we will be publishing a FAQ section on this page, so you can view them and clarify the ones that you have if they are the same as yours. We want to give you a good service with a good product, so you can tell other about us! We want to be present in all your events!
